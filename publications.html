Two and Four Wheels Differential Racing Bots

IEEE · Aug 2025
LalithKishore J, Harikrishna S, Guhan K.B, Deepak Kumar K, Akhil VM

Compares two-wheeled and four-wheeled racing robots in ROS-Gazebo using PWM control, kinematics, dynamics, and SLAM, with path planning via Euclidean, Manhattan, Chebyshev distances, and DTW. Four-wheeled robots offered better control; two-wheeled robots excelled in speed but lacked stability.

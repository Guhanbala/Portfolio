Two and Four Wheels Differential Racing Bots
IEEE Conference, 2025
Read paper ↗
Conducted a comparative analysis of two-wheeled and four-wheeled differential drive racing robots in a ROS-Gazebo simulation environment. Evaluated velocity, acceleration, torque distribution, and trajectory stability, demonstrating that four-wheeled robots reduced path deviation from 3.4% to 1.1%, while two-wheeled robots achieved higher peak speeds of 2.8 m/s and accelerations of 4.5 m/s².
